Create Login Form for Project-1





  • First register a username and password for an account, if you already have an account login with the same exact username and password you registered with.

  • Select "Play Game" to join a lobby with your friends.

  • Before you begin the game, all the player will make a wager that the winning player will recieve.
  • Then you will select how many places to be visited to win the game.

  • Once the game starts you will be given a list of places to visit, you select of the places for the route to that place. Once you reach that place you will be granted a point to your score and once you reach the max places you will be announced as the winner.

  • The winner will then recieve the reward of the wagger that the lobby decided on before the game started.

  • Make sure to KEEP UP!

First player to reach all 5 locations first wins!
